Antipasti

Our antipasti section is a symphony of flavors and textures that kickstart your culinary journey with gusto. From the simplicity of classic bruschetta to the sophistication of our Prosciutto-Wrapped Asparagus, these appetizers are a perfect prelude to your meal. Our commitment to quality and the finest ingredients shines through in each meticulously crafted dish, inviting you to savor the anticipation of what's to come. Dive into our antipasti selection and embark on a delicious adventure that sets the stage for an unforgettable dining experience.

Experience the Mediterranean's essence with our Caprese Salad, a refreshing blend of juicy tomatoes, velvety mozzarella, and fresh basil, kissed by extra virgin olive oil and balsamic glaze.

Start your meal on a crisp note with our Bruschetta, where toasted bread is generously topped with diced tomatoes, aromatic garlic, fragrant basil, and a drizzle of olive oil.

Our Garlic Bread is the perfect accompaniment to any meal, boasting warm, buttery slices infused with the irresistible aroma of roasted garlic, sure to delight your senses.
